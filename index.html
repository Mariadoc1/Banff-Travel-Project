Why Banff

Home to sparkling lakes and captivating wildlife

Banff is a resort town located in Banff National Park, in Alberta, Canada. It was Canada's first national park and is one of four adjacent national parks designated as the Canadian Rocky Mountain Parks UNESCO World Heritage Site. Banff is famous for it's snow-capped mountains, glistening lakes and enchanting wildlife, making it a popular tourist destination year-round.

The lively streets of downtown Banff are lined with outstanding restaurants, bars and shops. With a range of activities available for all age groups, interests and abilities, Banff is a great destination no matter the season.
